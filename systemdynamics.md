## How It Works

RSI Forge explores Collective Recursive Intelligence (CRI) —
a model where humans, AI agents, memory systems, and governance
layers recursively improve together rather than independently.

The system operates through iterative loops:

1. Observe  
   Agents and humans generate signals, ideas, or outputs.

2. Evaluate  
   Outputs are scored for coherence, usefulness, and alignment.

3. Improve  
   The network proposes modifications to prompts, memory, workflows, or behaviors.

4. Govern  
   Intent Bound Authorization (IBA) constrains unsafe or unauthorized transitions.

5. Evolve  
   The collective system reorganizes into higher-order cognitive structures.

The hypothesis:
hybrid human-AI intelligence may outperform isolated superintelligence
through coherence, diversity, and recursive coordination.
